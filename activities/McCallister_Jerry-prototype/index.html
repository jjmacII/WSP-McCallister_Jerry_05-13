Welcome to Big State Drug. We are a family owned pharmacy, soda fountain and grill that has been operating since 1948. We offer a wide range of services from filling perscriptions in our pharmacy to making burgers and your favorite flavor milkshakes in our old fashioned soda fountain and grill. In addition to being a full service pharmacy we offer special order items, compounded drugs, and delivery. We offer a large selection of durable medical equipment as well as over the counter medications, health and beauty aids. We also offer a wide array of gifts, collectables, greeting cards, and complementary gift wrapping. See the location link for hours and a map.
